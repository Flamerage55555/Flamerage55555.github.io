Our Story

The narrative of ALBAIK started in 1974 in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia's Jeddah when the late Shakour AbuGhazalah recognized the need for reasonably priced, excellent food that was delivered promptly, politely, and in a welcoming setting. Shakour penned the first chapter of ALBAIK using all of his hard-earned savings, making him the first person in Saudi Arabia to present the broasted chicken concept to the market.
